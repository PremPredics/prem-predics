Prem Predics is a 1v1 prediction game where two players predict every single match throughout an entire Premier League season (380 matches).

The Goal

The Player with the most Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) becomes the Prem Predics Ultimate Champion.

🎴 Playing Power, Curse & Super Cards

There are 4 types of Card:

  • 💪 Power Cards (Regular Deck) - Help you or hinder your opponent
  • 🖤 Curse Cards (Regular Deck) - Negative effects for your opponent
  • ✨ Super Cards (Premium Deck) - Powerful premium abilities
  • 🎮 Game Cards - Best-of-5 Minigames for Super Card rewards
