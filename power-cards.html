How To Play
There are 4 types of Card:
Power Cards
(Regular Deck)
Curse Cards
(Regular Deck)
Super Cards
(Premium Deck)
Game Cards
(Minigames)
🎴Power, Curse and Super Cards can help you or hinder your opponent. They add strategy and excitement to the game!
🎴Game Cards (7 Total) are best-of-5 Minigames drawn every 5 Gameweeks starting in GW1. Win to earn 1 Super Medal.
🎴Start with 1 Power Card + 1 Curse Card from your league's Regular Deck.
🏅Reach milestones to earn medals, each medal can be spent to draw 1 card from the Regular Deck.
Regular Deck: sized to the final league player count, keeping a balanced Power and Curse mix.
Premium Deck: 7 Super Cards — Drawn by spending Super Medals won from "Game Card" Minigames

Prem Predics is a private league prediction game where players predict every single match throughout an entire Premier League season (380 matches).

The Goal

The Player with the most Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) becomes the Prem Predics Ultimate Champion.

Playing Power, Curse & Super Cards

There are 4 types of Card:

  • Power Cards (Regular Deck) - Help you or hinder your opponent
  • Curse Cards (Regular Deck) - Negative effects for your opponent
  • Super Cards (Premium Deck) - Powerful premium abilities
  • Game Cards - Best-of-5 Minigames for Super Medal rewards
