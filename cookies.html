Cookies and local storage

Current Use

Prem Predics uses essential browser storage and authentication/session storage to keep you signed in, remember your session, and operate the website and app. This may include cookies, local storage, or similar technologies used by Supabase and the app.

Essential Storage

Essential storage is required for login, account security, league access, and core gameplay features. Without it, Prem Predics may not work properly.

Analytics And Advertising

Prem Predics does not currently use advertising cookies or third-party marketing trackers. If analytics, adverts, or non-essential cookies are added in future, this policy should be updated and consent controls should be added where required.

Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies and site data in your browser settings. On the Android app, storage may be cleared through Android app settings. Clearing storage may log you out or remove local preferences.

Contact

Questions about cookies or storage can be sent to support@prempredics.com.
