How To Request Deletion

To request deletion of your Prem Predics account, email support@prempredics.com from the email address linked to your account with the subject line: Delete my Prem Predics account.

What Will Be Deleted

Prem Predics will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise your account profile data, including your email-linked account profile, username, name fields, profile picture, and personal profile details.

League And Gameplay Records

Some league records may need to be retained or anonymised rather than fully removed, so league history, scoring integrity, and other players' records are not broken. This may include predictions, scores, card activity, medals, and leaderboard history.

Timing

Deletion requests will be handled as soon as reasonably possible. You may be asked to confirm ownership of the account before deletion is completed.

Before Public App Store Release

Prem Predics is not currently published on Google Play. Before public app store release, this page should be kept as the public account deletion URL required for app store data safety information.
