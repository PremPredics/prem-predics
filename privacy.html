Your data

What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how Prem Predics handles personal data when you use the website or app. Prem Predics is currently a private league prediction game for users aged 18 and over.

Data We Collect

  • Account data, including email address and authentication details.
  • Profile data, including username, first name, optional last name, optional nationality, favourite team, favourite colour, and optional profile picture.
  • League data, including league membership, join codes, league creator status, cards, medals, predictions, Star Man picks, scores, and leaderboard/statistics records.
  • Technical data needed to keep you signed in and operate the website or app.

How We Use Data

Data is used to create accounts, run private leagues, save predictions and Star Man selections, calculate scores, show league pages, support account access, and maintain the service.

Authentication And Storage

Prem Predics uses Supabase for authentication and database storage. Supabase may process account and usage data needed to provide login, session, database, and security functionality.

Profile Pictures And User Content

If you upload or set a profile picture or profile details, they may be visible to other players in your private leagues. Do not upload content you do not have permission to use.

Sharing

Prem Predics does not sell your personal data. Your league activity is shared with other members of your private league where needed for gameplay, such as predictions after lock, Star Man picks, leaderboard data, and card activity.

Retention

Account, league, and gameplay records may be kept while your account or league remains active. If you request account deletion, Prem Predics will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise your personal data unless it is needed for security, dispute handling, or legal reasons.

Your Rights

You can ask for access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To make a request, contact support@prempredics.com from the email address linked to your account.

Children

Prem Predics is for users aged 18 and over. Users under 18 must not create an account or use the service.

Unofficial Status

Prem Predics is an independent private league prediction game and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Premier League, its clubs, or its players.
