Private league rules

Overview

Prem Predics is a private league football prediction game. Players predict match scores, pick one Star Man each gameweek, play cards, earn medals, and compete for Ultimate Champion Points (UC Points).

Predictions

Players can predict match scores before the relevant lock time. A Correct Score earns 3 UC Points. A Correct Result earns 1 UC Point. Power and Curse cards may change how some predictions score.

Star Man

Players can choose one eligible footballer as their Star Man each gameweek. A Star Man normally earns 3 UC Points per goal, 1 UC Point per assist, -1 UC Point per yellow card, and -3 UC Points per red card. Card effects may alter this.

Cards

Power Cards help the player who plays them. Curse Cards hinder an opponent. Super Cards are premium effects earned through Super Medals. Game Cards are best-of-5 minigames that can award Super Medals.

Deadlines

Most predictions, Star Man picks, Power Cards, and Game Card entries lock 90 minutes before the first kick-off of the gameweek. Curse Cards must be played earlier. Some cards have special timing rules described on their card.

Manual Data Entry

Results and stats may be entered manually by a global administrator. If data is corrected, scores, leaderboards, medals, and card effects may update accordingly.

Fair Play

Do not exploit bugs, interfere with another user, create abusive usernames/profile content, or use Prem Predics to run gambling, betting, or paid-prize competitions.

The detailed gameplay guide on the How To Play page explains card effects and scoring in more detail. If there is a conflict, the current in-app card definition and scoring logic should be treated as the active rule.
