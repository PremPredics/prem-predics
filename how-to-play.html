How to Play - Premier League Prediction League

The Premier League Prediction League is a 1v1 competitive game where two players face off throughout an entire Premier League season (380 matches).

The Goal

Earn more Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) than your opponent by:

  • Predicting match outcomes correctly
  • Selecting a "Star Man" each week to earn bonus points
  • Playing Power & Curse cards strategically

Key Rules

  • ✅ All predictions must be made at least 1.5 hours before kickoff
  • ⭐ Each player selects a different Star Man each week (no duplicates)
  • 🎴 Start with 1 Power Card + 1 Curse Card from a 52-card regular deck
  • 🏆 Win "Game Card" mini-tournaments (every 5 gameweeks) to draw from the premium deck (7 premium cards)

UC pts are earned through match predictions and Star Man performance. Here's the breakdown:

Match Predictions

+3 pts Correct Score (exact prediction)
+1 pt Correct Result (right outcome, wrong score)

Star Man Performance (Weekly)

+3 pts Per Goal
+1 pt Per Assist
-1 pt Per Yellow Card
-3 pts Per Red Card

Example Scoring Scenario

Gameweek 5 - Your Star Man is Erling Haaland:
• You predicted 7 correct scores (+21 pts)
• You predicted 4 correct results (+4 pts)
• Haaland scores 2 goals (+6 pts)
• Haaland gets 1 yellow card (-1 pt)
Total: +30 UC pts

Note: Power & Curse cards can modify these points (multipliers, bonuses, penalties).

Each week, you select one player from the Premier League to be your "Star Man" — a special player who earns you bonus/penalty points based on their performance.

How It Works

  1. Each gameweek, you pick 1 player as your Star Man
  2. Their goals, assists, and cards are tracked for that week
  3. You earn/lose points based on their performance
  4. The next week, you can pick a different player

The Exclusivity Rule (IMPORTANT!)

Once ANY player in the league has been chosen as a Star Man by ANY participant, NO OTHER PLAYER can ever select that same player as their Star Man for the rest of the season.

For example:
• Gameweek 1: You pick Harry Kane as your Star Man
• From that point on: Your opponent can NEVER pick Harry Kane
• You can pick him again in future weeks (before he's used up)
• This creates strategic depth — choose wisely!

Strategy Tip

Choose high-performing players who consistently get goals and assists, while blocking your opponent from selecting them in future weeks!

Cards are special abilities that can help you or hinder your opponent. They add strategy and excitement to the game!

Card Overview

  • Regular Deck: 52 cards (30 Power + 22 Curse) — Start with 1 of each
  • Premium Deck: 7 exclusive Super Cards — Won via "Game Card" mini-tournaments
  • Game Card: A mini best-of-5 tournament every 5 gameweeks; winner draws a premium card

How to Earn Cards

  • 🎯 Start with 1 Power + 1 Curse card
  • 🏆 Reach milestones & earn medals to acquire more cards
  • 🥇 Win "Game Card" tournaments (every 5 gameweeks) to draw Premium cards
