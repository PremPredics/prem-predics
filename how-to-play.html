Prem Predics - How To Play

Prem Predics is a 1v1 prediction game where two players predict every single match throughout an entire Premier League season (380 matches).

The Goal

The Player with the most Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) becomes the Prem Predics Ultimate Champion. Earn more Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) than your opponent by:

✅ All predictions must be made at least 1.5hrs before Kick-Off

UC pts can be earned through match predictions, here's the breakdown:

Match Predictions
+3 pts Correct Score (exact outcome and score)
+1 pt Correct Result (correct outcome, wrong score)

Note: Power, Curse & Super Cards can modify these points (multipliers, bonuses, penalties).

⭐Select one Premier League Player each week to be your "Star Man"

Star Men can only be picked once and cannot be chosen again by either player for the rest of the season.

Star Men earn or lose you UC pts for the Gameweek they are chosen for based on their performance, here's the breakdown:

Star Man Performance (Weekly)
+3 pts Per Goal
+1 pt Per Assist
-1 pt Per Yellow Card
-3 pts Per Red Card

Note: Power, Curse & Super Cards can modify these points (multipliers, bonuses, penalties).

There are 4 types of Card:

  • Power Cards (Regular Deck)
  • Curse Cards (Regular Deck)
  • Super Cards (Premium Deck)
  • Game Cards (Minigames)

🎴 Power, Curse and Super Cards can help you or hinder your opponent. They add strategy and excitement to the game!

🎴 Game Cards (7 Total) are best-of-5 Minigames drawn every 5 Gameweeks starting in GW1. Win to earn 1 Super Medal.

🎴 Start with 1 Power Card + 1 Curse Card from a 52-card Regular Deck

🏆 Reach milestones to earn medals, each medal can be spent to draw 1 card from the Regular Deck

Regular Deck: 52 cards (30 Power Cards & 22 Curse Cards) — Start with 1 of each
Premium Deck: 7 Super Cards — Drawn by spending Super Medals won from "Game Card" Minigames

🏅 Medals can be spent to draw 1 Card from the Regular Deck for each Medal.
