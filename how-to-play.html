Prem Predics - How To Play

How It Works

Prem Predics lets you create a private league for 2-10 players and predict every Premier League match across the full 380-game season.

Alongside score predictions, players use Power Cards, play Curse Cards against opponents, spend Super Cards, compete in Game Cards, and choose one footballer each gameweek as their Star Man.

The Goal

The player with the most Ultimate Champion Points (UC pts) becomes the Prem Predics Ultimate Champion. Earn UC pts through accurate score predictions, smart Star Man picks, card strategy, and Game Card wins.

Prem Predics is an independent private league prediction game and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Premier League, its clubs, or its players.

How To Earn UC Points

Predictions lock 90 minutes before each fixture kicks off.

UC pts can be earned through match predictions. Here's the breakdown:

Match Predictions
3 3 UC Points per Correct Score (exact outcome and score)
1 1 UC Point per Correct Result (correct outcome, wrong score)

Power, Curse and Super Cards can modify these points with multipliers, bonuses or penalties.

Select one Premier League footballer each gameweek to be your Star Man.

You can only pick the same Star Man once per season. That restriction is personal to you, so other players in your league can still choose that footballer for themselves.

Star Men earn or lose you UC pts for the gameweek they are chosen for based on their performance. Here's the breakdown:

Star Man Performance (Weekly)
3 3 UC Points per Goal
1 1 UC Point per Assist
-1 -1 UC Point per Yellow Card
-3 -3 UC Points per Red Card

Power, Curse and Super Cards can modify these points with multipliers, bonuses or penalties.

Cards add the tactical layer: help yourself, disrupt an opponent, or turn a gameweek into something bigger.

There are 4 types of Card:

  • Power Cards (Regular Deck)
  • Curse Cards (Regular Deck)
  • Super Cards (Premium Deck)
  • Game Cards (Minigames)

Power, Curse and Super Cards can change predictions, Star Man scoring, card draws, restrictions, bonuses and penalties.

Curse order matters: If any fixture is affected by more than one Curse Card, then the most recently played Curse Card will take affect, voiding the effects of previous cards.

Game Cards (7 Total) are best-of-5 minigames drawn every 5 gameweeks starting in GW1. Win to earn 1 Super Medal.

Start with 1 Power Card + 1 Curse Card from your league's Regular Deck.

Reach milestones to earn medals. Each medal can be spent to draw 1 card from the Regular Deck.

Regular Deck: sized to the final number of active league players, with a balanced Power and Curse mix
Premium Deck: 7 Super Cards are drawn by spending Super Medals won from "Game Card" Minigames

Click on each card to view what it does!

Medals can be spent to draw 1 Card from the Regular Deck for each Medal.
