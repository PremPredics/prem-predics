Support

Contact Prem Predics

For support, privacy requests, account questions, or policy questions, email support@prempredics.com.

What To Include

  • The email address linked to your Prem Predics account.
  • Your username.
  • The private league name, if the question is about a league.
  • A clear description of the issue.

League Issues

If your question is about a private league, include the league name and explain what happened. League creators can help with league setup and members, while Prem Predics support handles site-wide manually entered scores, player stats, and game card results.

Unofficial Status

Prem Predics is an independent private league prediction game and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Premier League, its clubs, or its players.
