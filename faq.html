Prem Predics - FAQ

Quick answers for the private-league version of Prem Predics.

Do I need an account to play?

Yes. You can read How To Play without logging in, but predictions, Star Man picks, cards, leaderboards and stats are only available after you log in and join a private league.

Can I play without joining a league?

No. Prem Predics currently uses private leagues only. After logging in, create a league or join one with a join code.

When does a league lock?

A league stays open until the first match of that league's first gameweek kicks off. At that point the member count locks and the league uses the deck rules for that final player count.

When do predictions lock?

Score predictions lock 90 minutes before each match kicks off, unless a card changes your personal deadline.

When does Star Man lock?

Your Star Man pick locks 90 minutes before the first match of the current gameweek, unless a card such as Power of the Late Scout changes your personal deadline.

Can I pick the same Star Man twice?

No. Within a league, each player can only use a Premier League player as their Star Man once per season.

What are Regular and Premium cards?

Regular cards are Power and Curse cards. Premium cards are Super cards. You spend Regular Medals to draw Regular cards and Super Medals to draw Premium cards.

How long do Super cards last?

Super cards last 1 gameweek in 2-3 player leagues, 2 gameweeks in 4-6 player leagues, and 3 gameweeks in 7-10 player leagues.

How do Game Cards work?

Game Cards run over five gameweeks. Players submit the required prediction for each active week, the global admin enters the actual result, and the site ranks the league to award the winner.

Can anyone see my password?

No. Passwords are handled by Supabase Auth and are not visible as plain text in the app or database.
